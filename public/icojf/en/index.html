How We Work

A scoped workflow before implementation.

The process keeps projects small enough to launch, accept and maintain.

1

1. Scope review

Confirm business goal, existing systems, owner, budget range, launch window and first-release acceptance.

2

2. Prototype or API validation

Fix screens, data objects, field mapping, permissions, exception paths and client-side inputs.

3

3. Staged build

Deliver in accepted slices with demo environment, change notes, log notes and sample acceptance paths.

4

4. Launch and handover

Hand over source code, deployment docs, environment variables, runbook, acceptance checklist and walkthrough.
